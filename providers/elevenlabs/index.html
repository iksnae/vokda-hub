ElevenLabs is the provider that moved the conversation about what TTS could sound like. When they launched, the gap between their output and everything else was jarring. The rest of the industry has been closing that gap since — but ElevenLabs keeps moving too.

Voice Catalog

With over 3,000 voices, ElevenLabs has the largest catalog of any TTS provider. The voices span accents, ages, styles, and emotional registers in a way that no other provider has matched. More importantly, the top-tier voices (the studio catalog) are genuinely expressive — they convey character, not just words.

Models

ElevenLabs offers multiple model tiers:

  • Eleven Turbo v2 — Low latency for real-time applications (~100ms)
  • Eleven Multilingual v2 — 29 languages, highest multilingual quality
  • Eleven Flash — Sub-100ms, optimized for conversational AI
  • Eleven Studio — Highest quality, designed for content creation

Voice Cloning

Two cloning tiers:

  • Instant Voice Cloning — Upload audio, clone in seconds. Available on all paid tiers.
  • Professional Voice Cloning — Fine-tuned from 30 minutes+ of clean audio. Most accurate for complex voice preservation.

ElevenLabs’ cloning quality remains among the best available — voices retain idiosyncratic speech patterns, not just surface tonality.

Dubbing and Localization

ElevenLabs’ Dubbing product translates and re-voices audio/video content while maintaining speaker identity across languages. For content creators expanding to new language markets, this is a major feature that other providers don’t offer in the same form.

When to Use ElevenLabs

  • Narration and content creation — audiobooks, podcasts, explainer videos
  • Character voices — games, interactive fiction, branded personas
  • Multilingual content where voice consistency across languages matters
  • High-quality voice cloning for branded or personalized voice
  • Any application where voice expressiveness is the product

When to Look Elsewhere

  • Real-time latency under 80ms — Cartesia wins here
  • Domain-specific pronunciation — Deepgram Aura-2
  • High volume at lowest cost — open-source models
  • Simple utility voices — AWS Polly or Azure at lower price points
